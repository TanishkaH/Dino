What do you mean by Endangered?

Endangered meaning is that animal or those species are about to be extinct. Examples:

  • Dinosaurs
  • Broad Billed Parrots

What do you mean by Extinct?

extinct meaning is that animal or those species are dead and no longer there on this planet (earth). Examples:

  1. Tigers
  2. Macaws
  3. Ostiches
  4. Rhinocerus
  5. White Tigers
